PRICING/SESSION FEES

 SESSION FEE INCLUDES:

  • Pre - Session phone or email consultation (optional)
  • Use of a wide range of handmade beanies, wraps, throws, blankets and unique props
  • Facebook sneak peek.
  • Private online gallery with 20 fully edited high resolution images for you to make your digital image purchases. Your gallery will be available within two weeks from the date of your session. (See the demo gallery)
  • $150 credit for use on prints. See print pricing for more info.

Session fee is due at the time of booking to secure your date and current pricing. It is non-refundable.

Please note, this fee does NOT include any print, digital images or products and only covers the Photographer's time, talent and creativity.

SESSION FEE

Newborn – $250. 2-3 hours photoshoot.

Maternity – $150. 1 hour photoshoot.

Baby (6 weeks to 12 months)– $150. 1 hour photoshoot.

Kids (1yr onward) – $150 for one child, extra children $50 each. 1 hour photoshoot.

Cake smash – $150.

Family (up to 4 people only) – $150 ($50 for extra family member). 1 hour indoor or outdoor.

DIGITAL IMAGES

Professionally edited digital images are $45 each.
