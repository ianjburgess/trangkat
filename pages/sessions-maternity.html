Maternity FAQ’s

WHEN IS THE BEST TIME FOR A MATERNITY SESSION?

Maternity Photography is best done between 30 and 36 weeks. If you are carrying multiples around 6-7months is a good time. Although this may feel a little bit late, this is the ideal time to capture the perfect bump! The timing is different for everyone and we recommend that you don’t wait too late because you will never know when the baby will actually come.

CAN OTHER MEMBERS OF MY FAMILY ATTEND THE SESSION?

Of course! Husbands, children and pets are welcome and encouraged to participate in the maternity session also. It’s a special time for everyone!

WHERE DO MATERNITY SESSIONS TAKE PLACE?

Maternity sessions take place either in my natural light studio in Ardeer or on location around Melbourne and the surrounds. I have some great location suggestions, just depends on what works best for you!

WHAT DO I WEAR TO A MATERNITY SESSION?

Wardrobe is the key to a photo session.

Some of the popular clothing items are:
- Bandeau tops
- Lingerie
- Form fitting dresses
- Flowy maxi dresses
- Statement necklaces

IS HAIR AND MAKE UP INCLUDED IN THE PRICE?

No. We don’t offer hair and makeup as a service but are happy to recommend some great professionals.

WHAT ARE THE BEST TIME TO HAVE PHOTOS TAKEN?

If you are doing outdoor location photography, then sunrise and sunset are definitely the best times. The light is softer on the skin and the sky is more dramatic.

HOW LONG DOES THE SHOOT GO FOR?

Maternity sessions usually last for 1 hour. This can depend on our location and how much walking we need to do.

WHAT HAPPENS IF IT RAINS?

We do not need perfect weather to get great photos but if it is really pouring with rain on this date, then we will reschedule for a time that is convenient to both of us.

HOW LONG AFTER THE SESSION CAN I EXPECT TO SEE MY IMAGES?

Within the first 24 hour after the photo session there will be a sneak peak image with our logo on Facebook and Instagram that has been chosen at the session by you. Moreover, you will also receive a private online gallery within the first 24 hours after the session approx. 20 – 30 unedited images that you have 48 hours to choose photos. You will need to click on the favourite button on which image you would like me to edit. It will take me 2-3 weeks to provide you another online gallery with 5-10 edited images.

ORDERING PRINTS & CANVASES

We use a colour calibrated digital workflow and liaise closely with a professional printing company to ensure your prints are of the finest quality.

To buy prints you can simply log into your private online gallery and click on the cart for each photo you would like to be printed. It will then guide you through the print process where you can choose the print size.
