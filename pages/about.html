Hello! My name is Trang Burgess. Thanks for visiting my website!

I am a Professional Photographer, a Midwife of 6 years and a mother to a beautiful girl. My passion for photographing little ones came about soon after having my baby. As a midwife I have always loved caring for new mothers and babies and to now capture these precious moments, moments that can be shared and enjoyed by the whole family.

I provide a relaxed, fun and enjoyable atmosphere all in the comfort of my home studio in Ardeer, Victoria and surrounding outdoor locations.

I love to work with natural lighting to produce the most natural images and to allow comfort during the session.

Thanks for stopping by and I hope you enjoy browsing through the images in my portfolio and let me show you an experience where we will capture memories of your beautiful bump and little one, which you will cherish generations to come.

To arrange your photography session, shoot me an email via the contact form, or give me a call on 0467 299 885 - I'd love to hear from you!
