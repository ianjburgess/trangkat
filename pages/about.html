Hello! My name is Trang Burgess. Thank you for visiting my website!

I am a Professional Photographer, a Midwife of 6 years and a mother to a beautiful girl.

My passion for photographing little ones came about soon after having my baby. I have always loved caring for mothers and new babies and now I capture their precious moments that will tell their stories for many years to come. These can be shared and enjoyed by family and friends. Giving these moments to you is my greatest passion.

I love working with natural lighting to produce the most natural images. This also allow for a more comfortable session.

Thank you for stopping by and I hope you enjoy browsing the images in my portfolio. These will show you an experience where we will capture memories of your beautiful bump and little one, that you will be cherished for generations to come.

To arrange your photography session, shoot me an email via the contact form, or give me a call on 0467 299 885 - I'd love to hear from you!
