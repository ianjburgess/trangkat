Baby and Family FAQ’s

WHERE DO BABY & FAMILY PHOTOGRAPHY SESSIONS TAKE PLACE?

Baby and Family photography can be done in my natural light studio, or on location around Melbourne and the surrounds.

Depending on the location you would like for your backdrop, we will work together in determining what works best for you. I have lots of location suggestions, but feel free to suggest a particular spot you have in mind.

WHEN IS THE BEST TIME TO PHOTOGRAPH MY BABY?

The next best time to photograph babies if you missed the newborn session is 3 months old! Otherwise I recommend waiting to photography your baby between 6-8 months of age as this is when babies hit some really exciting milestones such as sitting up, which gives us more flexibility and posing options for your session.

ARE PROPS INCLUDED IN THE SESSION?

Yes, all props are included in the session. If there are any particular props you like or have seen on my site just let me know. You are also welcome to bring any keepsakes or heirlooms along to the session.

HOW LONG AFTER THE SESSION CAN I EXPECT TO SEE MY IMAGES?

There will be a sneak peak image on facebook that has been chosen by me. You will receive an online gallery with 20 edited images 2 weeks after your session.

ORDERING PRINTS & CANVASES

We use a colour calibrated digital workflow and liaise closely with a professional printing company to ensure your prints are of the finest quality.

To buy prints you can simply log into your private online gallery and click on the cart for each photo you would like to be printed. It will then guide you through the print process where you can choose the print size.
