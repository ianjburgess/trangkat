Deposit and Payment.


$100 deposit is due upon booking and is non-refundable. Deposit secures your session date and current pricing.  The remainder of session package is due before or at the time of session.

Newborn session is best done in the first 2 weeks. If you fail to contact us to schedule your session, your deposit is forfeited or is applied toward a future session which must be scheduled within 6 months from original booking date.

There are no refunds on the deposit if you change your mind after the baby arrives since your date has been reserved and held preventing other clients from booking. Refunds will be given in extreme cases and are at the discretion of Trang Kat Photography.


Payment.

Payments can be paid via cash or bank transfer.

The photographer will not begin design work or process orders until payment is complete.

In the event that client arrives more than 45 minutes late, photographer reserves the right to cancel the session with no refunds given.

Photographer reserves the right to reschedule due to illness, weather, equipment malfunction, or other circumstances beyond their control.
